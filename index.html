Our essential mission: to find heirs in the context of a succession

Vacant property without owner, verification of devolution, bank account or life insurance in escheat, location of beneficiaries, establishment of property rights ... Our job is to find heirs then to represent the heir whose rights are established and ensure that these rights are best defended.

The inheritance genealogist is a real “long-term” investigator (from a few months to a few years, in France and around the world). Researcher in the Coutot-Roehrig database but also in the national archives, the town halls of France or other countries, he is on the ground to reconstruct the puzzle of a family history.

Genealogy is defined as the study of family origins and history, the word genealogy comes from two Greek words—one meaning “race” or “family” and the other “theory” or “science.”
